Sole traders

A sole trader, also called a single proprietor, is the simplest form of business. One person owns all of the assets and is responsible for all debts. Many businesses such cafes, personal trainers and hair stylists operate as sole traders.

Features
  • simple and cheap to set up
  • one individual trades on their own
  • may operate under their own name or a registered business name
  • controls and manages the business
  • is responsible for all debts and liabilities.
